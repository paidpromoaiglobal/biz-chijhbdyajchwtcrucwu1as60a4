About Us

DEFENCE WORLD PHYSICAL COACHING CENTRE, KALGACHIA

Defence World Physical Coaching Centre (DWPCC) in Kalgachia, Assam, is a dedicated training institute committed to preparing young aspirants for careers in the Indian Armed Forces, Police, and other government defence services. Located conveniently near Nabajyoti College, our centre provides structured physical training, fitness drills, and PT sessions designed to meet the rigorous standards required for defence recruitment examinations.

At DWPCC Kalgachia, we believe every young man and woman from this region deserves a fair chance at serving the nation. Our experienced coaches work tirelessly, with sessions starting as early as 4:00 AM, ensuring our students develop the discipline, stamina, and physical strength needed to succeed. Whether you are preparing for the Indian Army, Assam Police, or other paramilitary forces, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

  • Open 6 days a week from 4:00 AM to 9:30 PM for maximum flexibility
  • Located near Nabajyoti College, Kalgachia – easily accessible for local students
  • Focused training programs aligned with real defence recruitment physical standards
